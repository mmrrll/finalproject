Experience

The Coconut Project

Jun 2018 - Jan 2020

This is a foundation that my sister and I co-created a long with a family friend who lives in Bangalore, India. Ava and I visited hotels along Kaanapali Beach asking for travel-size hygiene supplies. We then created kits that could be delievered to some orphanages in Bangalore, India.
I also did a personal project where I taught sewing lessons to kids in the community in exchange for fabric. I used this fabric to sew pillow case dresses for girls in Africa.

Lahaina Yatch Club: Junior Sailing

Summer 2018 & 2019

Summer Program that a restaurant did to teach local kids how to sail 10 ft open bic boats. I would instruct classes such as wind direction, knots, and parts of a boat. I also would provide safety in the open ocean as an instructor.

Love From Lahaina

2019 - Present

Business Owner of a screen printing and design company. We sell to local shops in Lahaina and Paia with Lahaina favored details. I have created and managed inventory, cash flow, and the organization of products.
