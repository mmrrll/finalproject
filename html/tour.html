- FUN FACTS -

The Eras Tour will span over 151 shows over 5 continents. In Novemeber of 2023, it was the highest grossing tour of all time. It is the first tour to crack $1 billion. Over 4.35 million tickets have been sold throughout 60 shows. During the Seattle shows in July, the dancing and screaming caused seismic activity that was equivilant to a 2.3 magnitude earthquake.
